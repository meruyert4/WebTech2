Famous Chinese Celebrities

Xiao Zhan

A popular actor and singer, Xiao Zhan gained fame for his role in the drama "The Untamed" and as a member of the boy band XNINE.

Van Ibo

An actor and singer, Van Ibo is known for his versatility and work in both Chinese films and television shows.

Yang Mi

A famous actress and singer, Yang Mi is known for her roles in popular dramas such as "Eternal Love" and "The Interpreter."

Zhan Ling He

Zhan Ling He is a rising star in the Chinese entertainment industry. Known for his roles in TV dramas and films, he has gained popularity for his charismatic performances and versatile acting skills.

Luhan

A former member of EXO, Luhan is now a successful solo artist and actor, known for his contributions to pop music and acting.

Zhao Lusi

Zhao Lusi is a popular Chinese actress known for her roles in dramas such as "Love of Thousand Years" and "The Romance of Tiger and Rose." She is celebrated for her charming performances and rising fame in the Chinese entertainment industry.

Q&A

The Great Wall of China is a historic series of fortifications built to protect ancient Chinese states and empires from invasions and raids.

Major festivals in China include the Chinese New Year, Mid-Autumn Festival, and Dragon Boat Festival, celebrated with unique customs and traditions.

Chinese calligraphy is a traditional art form that uses brush and ink to write characters, emphasizing aesthetic expression and cultural heritage.

China's natural wonders include Zhangjiajie National Forest Park, Jiuzhaigou Valley, and the breathtaking Yellow Mountains.

Traditional Chinese dishes include Peking Duck, Kung Pao Chicken, Dim Sum, and a variety of regional specialties.
